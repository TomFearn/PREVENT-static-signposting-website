Welcome to Safeguard

:Your Guide to the UK Government's PREVENT Scheme The UK government's PREVENT scheme is a crucial part of the nation's counter-terrorism strategy, designed to safeguard individuals and communities from the risk of radicalisation and extremist influence. Launched as part of the larger CONTEST strategy, PREVENT aims to identify and support those at risk of engaging in harmful ideologies or violent activities, providing early interventions that foster resilience and promote integration. On this site, you'll find accessible information about PREVENT's objectives, the types of support available, and how it impacts schools, workplaces, and community settings. Whether you are a professional, a parent, or a concerned citizen, this resource will help you understand how PREVENT operates, what it aims to achieve, and how it aligns with broader efforts to promote safety, equality, and inclusion across the UK. Explore the resources, learn about the referral process, and see how PREVENT works to protect and empower communities across the UK.
